Spacelift is compared with custom Internal Developer Platforms, noting that Spacelift functions as an IDP tailored specifically for infrastructure use cases, excelling at automation, governance, and drift detection without requiring months of platform engineering investment. Teams should choose Spacelift for focused infrastructure needs or build a custom IDP when handling multiple non-infrastructure workflows requiring a dedicated platform team.
