This article identifies nine key challenges organizations face when operating multicloud infrastructure across providers like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, including architectural complexity, fragmented observability, cost management difficulties, security inconsistencies, and skills gaps. The piece recommends unifying tooling through Infrastructure as Code and CI/CD platforms and leveraging tools like Spacelift for orchestration across multiple clouds to achieve the promised flexibility and cost benefits of multicloud strategies.
